Handouts!

A few of my handouts will be included here, and I hope to amass a large collection throughout the years. Most will in preparation for Mathcounts, MAA Contests (i.e. AMC 8, AMC 10, AIME, etc.), and other math contests (e.g. HMMT, ARML, PUMaC, etc.). Hope you enjoy! You may have noticed that the handouts are divided into categories. Here is a brief summary:
  1. Level 1: For Mathleague and Beginner Mathcounts and AMC 8 (and for those who live in Texas, TMSCA)
  2. Level 2: For AMC 10/12 and AIME (you could consider this "Pre-Olympiad")
  3. Level 2: For USA(J)MO
